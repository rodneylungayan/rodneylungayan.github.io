Imagine we're out on a footie field, playing catch together. It's a breeze, with the ball flying effortlessly between us. Good wholesome fun. Now, picture a towering apartment building standing between us. If you throw the ball, it's bound to bounce off the building and maybe even break a window. üòÖ

That's pretty much Fixed Wireless. Instead of two humans, we have our telecommunications towers and a dish on your roof, with the signal acting as the ball. When there's nothing obstructing the path, it's a direct line through the air, free from congestion or delay, delivering high-speed internet action. That's why Fixed Wireless is a fantastic alternative to the nbn‚Ñ¢, which passes the signal through numerous nodes, connection points, and cabling routes between your home and the tower. It's not exactly a thrilling game of catch, if you ask us.

Thankfully, our towers are great pitchers and more importantly our installers will ensure the best possible connection when they're setting up the dish on your roof. Once that's all set, the game begins, taking your internet experience to the next level.

Pentanet was founded by gamers. So if our most popular, fastest-growing connection option wasn't good for gamers, well, we'd hang our hats in shame. Fixed Wireless is superfast, low-latency and uber reliable. This makes it perfect whether you're clicking heads or cloud hopping with GeForce NOW Powered by CloudGG.

Our Wi-Fi setup service is the ultimate way to get your home ready to enjoy the best connection possible. The Omega service is included free of charge with your installation, seeing our subscriber success expert visit your home to help find the optimal location for your router. They'll also setup a few devices for you, so you can get to enjoying the important stuff (Netflix üòú) right away. Higher tiers of the service will see them stick around as long as you need, setting up devices and walking you through your new connection and user portal.

Adding Fixed Wireless to a rental property is a win-win for everyone involved. Tenants can delight in superfast internet, indulging in uninterrupted streaming and gaming sessions. For landlords, it provides a competitive edge, allowing you to market the property as high-speed internet ready. The best part is that Fixed Wireless is available for all property types. As long as there's a clear line-of-sight to one of our towers, we can make the connection happen.
