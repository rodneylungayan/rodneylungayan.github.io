Overall, neXus represents a remarkable step forward for Perth's digital landscape. By blending the potential of Meta's Terragraph technology with Team Pentanet's innovative approach, this project has the potential to transform the way people connect, work, and live in the city, positioning Perth as a leader in digital infrastructure and connectivity.

Initially designed for dense urban landscapes, our team embarked on an extensive research and development journey to develop a solution to fit Perth's unique environment. We created our Fixed Wireless network to thrive where the nbn‚Ñ¢ fell short, and neXus is the next evolution of our mission for Perth's digital future.

How neXus works is by turning every home connected to the service into a miniature Fixed Wireless tower, or as we like to call them, meshy bois. By creating a web of these meshy bois, we create a diverse pathway of connection routes. If your home doesn‚Äôt have a direct pathway back to the tower (the spider at the centre of its web), it can bounce down and around these pathways between meshy bois to find the fastest route possible.

Gone are the days of relying on a clear line of sight to a tower. With neXus, we deliver practically unrivalled speeds. This is because wireless connections thrive on proximity. While normal wireless signals degrade with distance, neXus ensures that your data only needs a few short hops between our meshy bois, outperforming a single long leap between you and the tower. This lets you enjoy blazing fast next-level wireless speeds like nothing Perth ever dreamed of.
